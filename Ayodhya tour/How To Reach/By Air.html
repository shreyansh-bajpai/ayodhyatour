How To Reach Ayodhya

By Air

Since Ayodhya does not have an airport, one has to reach either Chaudhary Charan Singh Airport at Amausi in Lucknow,

which is at a distance of 134 km or Bamrauli Airport in Prayagraj which is about 166 km away.
